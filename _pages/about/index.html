I am Panagiotis, an M.Sc. Computer Science student at the Technical University of Munich. My passion lies in Computer Vision, and I am particularly interested in State Representation research. Specifically, I explore ways to enhance latent representations and embeddings by incorporating additional objective functions, such as masked reconstruction of specific object parts. My goal is to improve the robustness of the original task, including but not limited to RGB reconstruction.

My goal through my research and projects is to connect with people, both to offer and to learn from them, and ideally, to leave a positive impact.
